?How much is the ticket price for Disneyland Paris

The fastest, best and cheapest way to buy Disneyland Paris tickets is to buy from Disneyland's own website. If you have an international credit or non-credit card, you can buy your ticket easily by going to this link. You can save the purchased ticket on your phone or print it out and take it there with you. Online tickets are usually cheaper than the tickets you find at the Disneyland ticket office.


?Where can I buy Disneyland Paris tickets

Don't worry if you don't have a bank card, there are usually other ways. Buying Disneyland tickets at the entrance of the Disneyland complex can be done with a long queue. But in this case, you have to pay the full price of the ticket. but do not worry! There are some travel agencies and some big stores in Paris that buy Disneyland tickets in a very large number at a lower price than the Disneyland Paris ticket price in the Disneyland park itself and provide them to their customers.


?Is it possible to buy Disneyland tickets online

If for any reason you are not able to buy online or buy discounted tickets, go directly to Disneyland itself and after passing through the security check line, join the long line of customers in front of the ticket counters.
